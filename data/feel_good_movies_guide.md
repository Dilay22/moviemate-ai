# Feel-Good Movies Guide

Feel-good movies are uplifting, funny, hopeful, or motivational. They are good when users want to improve their mood or watch something positive.

Common feel-good genres:
- Comedy
- Family adventure
- Inspirational drama
- Light romance
- Animation

Examples:
- Legally Blonde: funny, confident, and motivational
- The Pursuit of Happyness: inspiring but emotional
- School of Rock: funny and energetic
- Mamma Mia!: musical, colorful, and cheerful
- Chef: warm and satisfying

If the user wants pure happiness, avoid movies with tragic endings, heavy conflict, or disturbing themes.
