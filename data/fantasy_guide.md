# Fantasy Movie Guide

Fantasy movies are good for users who want magical worlds, imagination, adventure, myths, creatures, or fairy-tale elements. They can be cozy, epic, romantic, or dark depending on the story.

Fantasy styles:
- Epic fantasy: large worlds, quests, battles
- Cozy fantasy: magical but gentle and comforting
- Dark fantasy: magic with danger or horror elements
- Family fantasy: safe, colorful, and accessible

Examples:
- The Princess Bride: fantasy adventure with comedy and romance
- Stardust: romantic fantasy adventure
- Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone: magical school adventure
- The Lord of the Rings: epic fantasy and adventure
- Spirited Away: animated fantasy with emotional depth

Choose lighter fantasy for relaxing nights and epic fantasy for viewers who want a bigger adventure.
