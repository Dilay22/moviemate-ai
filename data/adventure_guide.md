# Adventure Movie Guide

Adventure movies are good for users who want journeys, discoveries, danger, exploration, and exciting stories. They often work well for families and groups.

Adventure styles:
- Family adventure: safe and exciting
- Fantasy adventure: magical journeys
- Action adventure: danger and stunts
- Historical adventure: quests or exploration in past settings

Examples:
- The Princess Bride: fantasy adventure with humor
- Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade: action adventure
- Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle: funny group adventure
- The Goonies: classic group adventure
- Moana: animated adventure and music

Adventure is a safe choice when the user wants something fun, energetic, but not too dark.
