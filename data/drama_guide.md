# Drama Movie Guide

Drama movies focus on emotions, relationships, personal struggles, and character development. They are good for users who want meaningful stories rather than fast action or comedy.

Drama styles:
- Emotional drama: deep feelings and serious themes
- Inspirational drama: growth, success, and hope
- Family drama: relationships and personal conflict
- Historical drama: realistic stories set in the past

Examples:
- The Pursuit of Happyness: emotional and inspiring
- Little Women: warm family drama
- The Social Network: modern biographical drama
- Good Will Hunting: personal growth and emotional depth
- Hidden Figures: inspiring historical drama

Avoid heavy drama if the user wants something funny, relaxing, or not sad.
