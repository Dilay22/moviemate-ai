# Group Movie Night Guide

For group movie nights, movies should be entertaining, easy to follow, and not too slow. The best choices usually create reactions, laughter, suspense, or discussion.

Good group movie genres:
- Comedy
- Action comedy
- Mystery
- Adventure
- Light thriller
- Animation

Examples:
- Game Night: funny and energetic for groups
- Knives Out: mystery and comedy, good discussion movie
- Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse: energetic and visually exciting
- Ocean's Eleven: stylish, fun, and easy to watch
- Shrek: funny and familiar

Avoid very slow dramas or confusing experimental films unless the group specifically wants something serious.
