# Science Fiction Movie Guide

Science fiction movies explore technology, space, artificial intelligence, time travel, future societies, or scientific possibilities. They are good for users who enjoy imagination, ideas, and futuristic worlds.

Sci-fi styles:
- Space sci-fi: planets, space travel, aliens
- AI sci-fi: robots, artificial intelligence, human-machine relationships
- Dystopian sci-fi: future societies with problems or control
- Time travel sci-fi: timelines, paradoxes, alternate realities

Examples:
- Interstellar: emotional space science fiction
- The Martian: survival, science, and humor
- Arrival: thoughtful alien communication story
- Ex Machina: artificial intelligence and ethics
- WALL-E: animated sci-fi, emotional and family-friendly

For casual groups, choose lighter sci-fi. For serious viewers, choose deeper philosophical sci-fi.
