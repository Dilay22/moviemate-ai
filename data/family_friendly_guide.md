# Family-Friendly Movie Guide

Family-friendly movies are suitable for mixed audiences and avoid extreme violence, explicit content, heavy horror, or overly dark themes. They are useful when users want a safe choice for parents, siblings, or younger viewers.

Good family-friendly genres:
- Animation
- Adventure
- Light comedy
- Fantasy
- Musical

Examples:
- Paddington 2: wholesome, funny, and warm
- Ratatouille: cozy and inspiring
- Finding Nemo: adventure and emotional family story
- The Princess Bride: fantasy adventure with comedy
- Matilda: magical, funny, and charming

Avoid:
- horror
- dark psychological thrillers
- graphic violence
- extremely sad dramas
