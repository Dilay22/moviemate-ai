# Action Movie Guide

Action movies are best for users who want energy, excitement, fights, chases, stunts, and fast pacing. They work well for groups who want something entertaining and easy to follow.

Action styles:
- Pure action: fights, explosions, and chase scenes
- Action comedy: exciting but funny
- Superhero action: powers, villains, and big visual scenes
- Adventure action: travel, danger, and discovery

Examples:
- Mission: Impossible - Fallout: intense action and stunts
- Mad Max: Fury Road: fast, visual, and high-energy
- John Wick: stylish action, but violent
- The Fall Guy: action comedy with humor and stunts
- Guardians of the Galaxy: superhero action with comedy

Avoid very violent action movies if the user asks for family-friendly, cozy, or low-stress movies.
