# Musical Movie Guide

Musicals are best for users who enjoy songs, dancing, expressive emotions, colorful scenes, and theatrical storytelling. They can be romantic, funny, dramatic, or family-friendly.

Musical styles:
- Romantic musical: love story with songs
- Feel-good musical: colorful and cheerful
- Dramatic musical: emotional or intense
- Family musical: accessible and fun for many ages

Examples:
- Mamma Mia!: cheerful, colorful, and fun
- La La Land: romantic and emotional musical
- The Greatest Showman: energetic and inspirational
- Singin' in the Rain: classic comedy musical
- Encanto: animated family musical

Avoid musicals if the user says they dislike singing or wants a serious realistic story.
