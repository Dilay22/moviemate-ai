# Horror Movie Guide

Horror movies are best for users who want fear, suspense, tension, and adrenaline. They are usually not suitable for relaxing nights, family movie nights, or users who ask for something light.

Horror subgenres:
- Supernatural horror: ghosts, demons, curses
- Psychological horror: fear based on the mind, paranoia, confusion
- Slasher horror: a killer or monster chasing characters
- Creature horror: monsters, animals, aliens, or unknown creatures

Examples:
- A Quiet Place: tense, suspenseful, not overly graphic
- Get Out: psychological horror with social themes
- The Conjuring: supernatural and scary
- Scream: horror with mystery and meta-comedy

Avoid horror if the user says:
- not scary
- relaxing
- cozy
- family-friendly
- anxious
- light mood
