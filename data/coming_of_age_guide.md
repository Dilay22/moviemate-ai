# Coming-of-Age Movie Guide

Coming-of-age movies focus on growing up, identity, friendship, first love, school, family, and self-discovery. They are often emotional, funny, nostalgic, or comforting.

Coming-of-age styles:
- Teen comedy: school, friendship, humor
- Emotional coming-of-age: personal struggles and growth
- Romantic coming-of-age: first love and identity
- Family coming-of-age: growing up within family relationships

Examples:
- Lady Bird: emotional, funny, and realistic
- The Way Way Back: sweet, warm, and personal
- The Perks of Being a Wallflower: emotional and thoughtful
- Booksmart: funny friendship comedy
- 10 Things I Hate About You: teen romance and comedy

Avoid very emotional coming-of-age films if the user asks for something purely cheerful.
