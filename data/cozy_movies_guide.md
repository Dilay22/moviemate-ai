# Cozy Movies Guide

Cozy movies are calm, comforting, warm, and low-stress. They are good for rainy days, relaxing evenings, tired students, or users who want something soft and safe.

Cozy movie traits:
- Warm atmosphere
- Low violence
- Gentle humor
- Hopeful ending
- Comfortable pacing
- Not too scary or intense

Examples:
- Paddington 2: wholesome, funny, and comforting
- Ratatouille: warm, food-centered, inspiring
- The Intern: calm and light
- Little Women: emotional but warm
- Chef: food, travel, friendship, and creativity

Avoid horror, dark thrillers, and extremely sad dramas when the user asks for cozy movies.
