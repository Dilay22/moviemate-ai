# Movies Under 2 Hours

Movies under 2 hours are good for casual watching, group movie nights, and users who do not want a long commitment.

Recommended examples:
- Game Night: comedy mystery, about 100 minutes
- Knives Out: mystery comedy, about 130 minutes, slightly over 2 hours depending on version
- The Grand Budapest Hotel: stylish comedy, about 100 minutes
- Mean Girls: comedy, about 97 minutes
- Shrek: animated comedy, about 90 minutes
- A Quiet Place: thriller horror, about 90 minutes
- The Devil Wears Prada: comedy-drama, about 109 minutes

When users request under 2 hours, prioritize movies around 90 to 115 minutes.
