# Thriller Movie Guide

Thriller movies are built around suspense, tension, mystery, danger, and uncertainty. They are good for users who want an exciting story without necessarily watching horror.

Thriller styles:
- Mystery thriller: solving secrets or crimes
- Psychological thriller: unreliable characters and mind games
- Action thriller: danger, chase scenes, and fast pacing
- Crime thriller: investigations, criminals, and moral conflict

Examples:
- Knives Out: mystery thriller, funny and stylish
- Gone Girl: psychological thriller, dark and intense
- The Prestige: mystery, rivalry, and twists
- Shutter Island: psychological mystery, intense mood
- Ocean's Eleven: crime thriller, stylish and fun

Avoid dark thrillers if the user asks for something relaxing, light, or family-friendly.
