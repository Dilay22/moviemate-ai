# Documentary Movie Guide

Documentaries are best for users who want to learn something real. They can focus on nature, history, technology, crime, sports, food, or social issues.

Documentary styles:
- Nature documentary: animals, environment, and visuals
- True crime documentary: investigations and real cases
- Technology documentary: inventions, AI, internet, or science
- Sports documentary: athletes, competition, and motivation
- Food documentary: cooking, culture, and travel

Examples:
- Free Solo: intense sports documentary
- Jiro Dreams of Sushi: food, craft, and discipline
- 13th: social and historical documentary
- The Social Dilemma: technology and social media
- My Octopus Teacher: nature and emotional connection

Avoid heavy documentaries if the user wants something light, fictional, or relaxing.
