# Comedy Movie Guide

Comedy movies are best for users who want a light, funny, and relaxing experience. They are good for friend groups, casual movie nights, and situations where the audience does not want something emotionally heavy.

Recommended comedy styles:
- Light comedy for relaxing nights
- Action comedy for groups who want energy
- Romantic comedy for date nights
- Family comedy for mixed audiences

Examples:
- Game Night: good for friend groups, funny and fast-paced
- The Intern: calm comedy-drama, suitable for relaxing evenings
- Legally Blonde: energetic, funny, and motivational
- Paddington 2: cozy, wholesome, and family-friendly

Avoid dark comedy if the user asks for something simple, family-friendly, or relaxing.
