# Movies to Avoid by Mood

Movie recommendations should avoid mismatching the user's mood. If a user says they want something relaxing, avoid intense horror, dark thrillers, and emotionally heavy dramas.

Avoid by mood:
- Relaxing or cozy: avoid horror, intense thrillers, disturbing dramas
- Funny: avoid slow serious dramas unless comedy-drama is acceptable
- Family-friendly: avoid graphic violence, adult themes, horror
- Date night: avoid extremely depressing movies unless requested
- Group night: avoid very slow or confusing experimental films
- Emotional but not sad: avoid tragic endings
- Not scary: avoid horror and intense suspense

The agent should explain avoided categories politely and suggest safer alternatives.
