# Movies Under 90 Minutes

Short movies are useful when users have limited time or want a quick movie night. These movies should be easy to start and finish without feeling too heavy.

Good short movie traits:
- Fast pacing
- Simple story
- Clear genre
- Not too emotionally exhausting

Examples:
- Toy Story: animated, funny, family-friendly, about 81 minutes
- Before Sunset: romantic drama, dialogue-focused, about 80 minutes
- My Neighbor Totoro: cozy animation, about 86 minutes
- Zombieland: horror comedy, about 88 minutes
- The Lion King: animation and adventure, about 88 minutes

Short movies are useful for weeknights, study breaks, and casual plans.
