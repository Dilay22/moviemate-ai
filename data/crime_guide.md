# Crime Movie Guide

Crime movies focus on criminals, investigations, heists, law enforcement, justice, or moral conflict. They can be serious, stylish, funny, or suspenseful.

Crime styles:
- Heist movie: planning and stealing something
- Detective crime: solving a case
- Gangster movie: organized crime and power
- Crime comedy: criminal events with humor

Examples:
- Ocean's Eleven: stylish and fun heist movie
- Catch Me If You Can: crime comedy-drama
- The Departed: serious crime thriller
- Baby Driver: action crime with music
- Inside Man: smart heist thriller

Avoid dark crime films if the user wants cozy, family-friendly, or low-stress recommendations.
