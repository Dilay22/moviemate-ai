# Date Night Movie Guide

Date night movies should match the mood of the people watching. Good choices are often romantic, funny, cozy, or visually beautiful without being too stressful.

Good date night options:
- Romantic comedy for light and fun energy
- Cozy drama for calm evenings
- Stylish mystery for conversation
- Animated or fantasy movies for playful moods

Examples:
- Crazy Rich Asians: romantic comedy with glamour
- About Time: romance with emotional warmth
- La La Land: romantic, musical, visually beautiful
- The Grand Budapest Hotel: stylish and funny
- Before Sunrise: romantic and conversation-focused

Avoid:
- extremely scary horror
- very depressing dramas
- disturbing psychological thrillers
unless the user specifically asks for them.
