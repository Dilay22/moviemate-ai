# Romance Movie Guide

Romance movies focus on relationships, emotions, love, connection, and personal growth. They are good for date nights, emotional evenings, or users who want something warm and heartfelt.

Romance styles:
- Romantic comedy: light, funny, and sweet
- Emotional romance: deeper and sometimes sad
- Period romance: historical setting and dramatic love stories
- Teen romance: young love and coming-of-age themes

Examples:
- Crazy Rich Asians: glamorous romantic comedy
- 10 Things I Hate About You: funny teen romance
- Pride and Prejudice: classic period romance
- La La Land: romantic musical with emotional themes
- Before Sunrise: dialogue-focused romantic drama

Avoid very sad romance if the user wants something cheerful, cozy, or low-stress.
