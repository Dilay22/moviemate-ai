# Mystery Movie Guide

Mystery movies are best for users who enjoy clues, secrets, puzzles, investigations, and plot twists. They are good for groups because people can guess the answer together.

Mystery styles:
- Detective mystery: solving a crime or case
- Cozy mystery: lighter mystery without extreme violence
- Dark mystery: serious, tense, or disturbing
- Comedy mystery: mystery mixed with humor

Examples:
- Knives Out: funny, stylish detective mystery
- Murder on the Orient Express: classic detective mystery
- Enola Holmes: lighter mystery with adventure
- The Prestige: mystery with rivalry and twists
- Searching: modern mystery told through screens

Avoid dark mysteries if the user asks for something light, cozy, or family-friendly.
