# Superhero Movie Guide

Superhero movies focus on heroes, powers, villains, action, and moral choices. They are good for viewers who want excitement, humor, visual effects, and big stories.

Superhero styles:
- Light superhero: funny and colorful
- Dark superhero: serious and intense
- Team superhero: group of heroes working together
- Origin story: how a hero begins

Examples:
- Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse: colorful, funny, and creative
- Guardians of the Galaxy: action, comedy, and found family
- The Dark Knight: darker superhero crime thriller
- Wonder Woman: action adventure with heroic themes
- Iron Man: origin story with humor and technology

Avoid darker superhero films if the user wants something cozy, gentle, or family-safe.
